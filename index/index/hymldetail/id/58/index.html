广西壮族自治区第四地质队

The Fourth Geological Team of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region

 

是一个集固体矿产勘查开发、区域地质调查、水文地质、工程勘察与施工、地形测绘等为一体的综合性地质勘查大队。拥有各类专业技术人员471人,其中教授级高级职称3人，高级技术职称112人，中级技术职称234人，初级技术职称122人。拥有固体矿产勘查，区域地质调查，水文地质、工程地质、环境地质调查，液体矿产勘查，地质钻探等10项甲级、8项乙级、1项丙级勘查资质。拥有国内外先进的技术设备和配套的技术服务。主营区域地质调查，矿产、水文、工程、环境地质勘查，地球物理、地球化学勘查，岩矿鉴定与测试，地质灾害评估与防治、工程勘察与施工、矿山开发等综合地质业务。 

The Fourth Geological Team of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region is a comprehensive geological exploration team that integrates solid mineral exploration and development, regional geological survey, hydrogeology, engineering survey and construction, terrain surveying and mapping, etc. The Team has 471 professional and technical personnel of various types, including 3 professional-level senior technical personnel, 112 senior technical personnel, 234 intermediate technical personnel, and 122 junior technical personnel. It has 10 Grade A, 8 Grade B, and 1 Grade C exploration qualifications, including solid mineral exploration, regional geological survey, hydrogeology, engineering geology, environmental geology survey, liquid mineral exploration, and geological drilling. It has advanced technical equipment and supporting technical services both domestically and internationally. The main businesses include regional geological survey, mineral resources, hydrological, engineering and environmental geological exploration, geophysical and geochemical survey, rock and mineral identification and testing, geological disaster assessment and prevention, engineering survey and construction, and mine development.

建队以来，先后为国家提交各类地质勘查成果报告600多份。发现和探明了金、银、锰、铝土矿、膨润土矿等20多个矿种270多处矿床，其中特大型矿床2个、大型矿床34个、中型矿床40多个及大量的小型矿床；提交黄金金属资源储量114.3吨、银金属资源储量1535吨、铅+锌金属资源储量80万吨、钨金属资源储量4万吨、锰矿1.57亿吨、铝土矿5.9亿吨、膨润土9.83亿吨、煤矿4.62亿吨、铁矿5000万吨、五氧化二钒153万吨以上。完成了大明山、西大明山测区钦杭成矿带西段、安然背斜等重要成矿带共18个图幅的1:5万区域调查工作。先后荣获国务院、地矿部“全国功勋地质队”，国土资源部“全国模范地勘单位”、“全国模范职工之家”、“国土资源系统功勋集体”，国家地质总局、自治区“大庆式企业”，自治区“地质勘查先进单位”、“文明单位”等荣誉称号30多项（次）。所做项目荣获国土资源部“全国地质勘查行业优秀地质找矿项目”、“国土资源科学技术奖”、广西壮族自治区人民政府“广西科学技术进步奖”等省（区）部级以上技术成果奖50多项，其中一等奖3项、二等奖4项、三等奖40多项。培养并涌现了“无私奉献的探宝功臣”李正海和一批以李正海同志为代表的“生命不息、找矿不止”的先进模范人物。 近年来，该队注重实施“走出去，请进来”战略，充分发挥自身技术优势，在国际地勘项目合作中拓展业务，先后与加拿大、澳大利亚、印度尼西亚、柬埔寨等国家的矿业公司进行过广泛的经济合作和技术交流，建立了友好的合作伙伴关系，在东南亚部分国家开拓了地质业务技术工作。

Since its establishment, the Team has submitted over 600 reports on various geological exploration results to the country. It discovered and explored over 20 types of mineral deposits, including gold, silver, manganese, bauxite, bentonite, and more than 270 mineral deposits, including 2 super large deposits, 34 large deposits, over 40 medium deposits, and a large number of small deposits; submitted gold metal resource reserves of 114.3 tons, silver metal resource reserves of 1,535 tons, lead-zinc metal resource reserves of 800,000 tons, tungsten metal resource reserves of 40,000 tons, manganese of 157 million tons, bauxite of 590 million tons, bentonite of 983 million tons, coal mine of 462 million tons, iron ore of 50 million tons, and vanadium pentoxide of over 1.53 million tons. The Team completed eighteen 1:50000 regional survey of important metallogenic belts, including the western part of the Qinzhou-Hangzhou metallogenic belt and the Anran anticline in the Daming Mountain and the West Daming Mountain survey areas. It has successively won more than 30 honorary titles, such as the “National Meritorious Geological Team” awarded by the State Council and then Ministry of Geology and Mineral Resources, the “National Model Geological Exploration Unit”, the “National Model Workers’ Home”, the “Meritorious Collective of the Land and Resources System” awarded by then Ministry of Land and Resources, the “Daqing-style Enterprise” awarded by then National Geological Administration and Guangxi, the “Advanced Institution of Geological Exploration” and the “Civilized Institution” awarded by Guangxi. The projects implemented by the Team have won more than 50 important technical achievement awards, including 3 first prizes, 4 second prizes, and more than 40 third prizes, such as the “National Excellent Geological Prospecting Project in the Geological Exploration Industry”, the “Land and Resources Science and Technology Award”, and the “Guangxi Science and Technology Progress Award”. Li Zhenghai, a selfless hero in treasure exploration, and a group of advanced model figures represented by him were cultivated and emerged. In recent years, the team has focused on the “go global and bring in” strategy, leveraging its technical advantages to expand international geological exploration project cooperation business. It has cooperated and exchanged with overseas mining companies in Canada, Australia, Indonesia, Cambodia and other countries, established friendly partnerships, and expanded geological business and technical work in some Southeast Asian countries.

安哥拉锰矿考察

Investigation of manganese mine in Angola

 

矿产勘查

Mineral exploration

 

山西柳林县煤层气水平开发井项目，是该队下属广西第一地质工程公司首次采用CMD150S模块式顶驱多功能钻机、首次应用水平井定（导）向钻井新技术施工的项目，开孔口径为345.1mm终孔口径为171.4mm，公司自2020年9月至2021年12月共计施工5口钻井，主眼有效进尺达7849m， 导眼进尺达2717m。应用随钻双伽玛定（导）向钻井技术，精在定向引导井眼轨迹，达到控制井眼轨迹，水平井煤层钻遇牢满足地质设计要求，工程质量验收合格，获得了业主、监理的一致好评。该项目的顺利实施，锻炼了公司钻井作业队伍，提升了钻井技术水平，提高了市场竞争能力，为服务大地质及清洁能源的勘探开发奠定了基础。

In the horizontal development well project of coalbed methane in Liulin County, Shanxi Province, it is the first time that Guangxi No.1 Geological Engineering Company, a subsidiary of the Team, used CMD150S modular top-drive multifunctional drilling rig and applied the new technology of directional (oriented) drilling of horizontal wells. The opening hole diameter was 345.1mm and the final hole diameter was 171.4mm. From September 2020 to December 2021, the company conducted five drilling holes in total, with the effective drilling rate of progress of the main hole reaching 7849m and drilling rate of progress of the oriented hole reaching 2717m. The dual gamma directional drilling technology while drilling can directionally guide the well borehole trajectory and achieve the control of borehole trajectory, the horizontal well meets the geological design requirements firmly, and the project is qualified, which has been highly praised by customer and supervisor. The smooth implementation of this project has trained the drilling operation team of the company, improved the drilling technology level, improved the market competitiveness, and laid a foundation for serving the exploration and development of large geology and clean energy.
