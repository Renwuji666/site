广西地矿建设集团有限公司

Guangxi Geological and Mineral Construction Group Co., Ltd.

广西地矿建设集团有限公司是一家集投资开发、工程施工、技术服务等业务于一体的国有企业。具有建筑工程施工总承包壹级、地基基础工程专业承包壹级、建筑装修装饰工程专业承包壹级；房地产开发贰级、公路工程施工总承包贰级、市政公用工程施工总承包贰级、环保工程专业承包贰级；地质灾害、工程勘察专业类岩土工程甲级等20多项资质。现有员工518人，其中专业技术人才301人、中高级人才215人（含正高级职称4人、高级职称77人、各类注册师148人）。

Guangxi Geological and Mineral Construction Group Co., Ltd. is a state-owned enterprise integrating investment and development, engineering construction and technical services. The company has more than 20 qualifications, including Grade A in general contracting of building engineering, Grade A in professional contracting of foundation engineering and Grade A in professional contracting of building decoration engineering; Grade B in real estate development, Grade B in general contracting of highway engineering construction, Grade B in general contracting of municipal public works construction and Grade B in professional contracting of environmental protection engineering; Grade A in geotechnical engineering for geological disasters and engineering investigation. The Group has 518 employees, including 301 professional and technical personnel and 215 middle and senior personnel (including 4 professor-level senior personnel, 77 senior personnel and 148 different types of registrars).

多年来，集团紧跟国家、广西发展战略，在“一带一路”、“乡村振兴”、“绿色生态”、“北部湾经济区建设”等领域承担实施一系列标志性工程，数十个项目分别荣获国家级、省级质量和安全文明施工类奖项，领先同侪的关键技术、客户为核的经营理念和诚信至善的服务品质，造就企业卓尔不凡的核心竞争力。

Over the years, following the development strategies of the country and Guangxi, the Group has undertaken and implemented a series of landmark projects in the fields of “The Belt and Road”, “Rural Revitalization”, “Green Ecology” and “Beibu Gulf Economic Zone Construction”, and dozens of projects have won national and provincial awards for quality and safe and civilized construction. The company’s outstanding core competitiveness has been created by leading its peers in its key technologies, customer-oriented business philosophy, and honest and refined service quality.

总部位于中国南宁市的公司总部办公大楼，已于2023年建成进驻

The headquarters was completed in 2023 in Nanning, China.

公司旗下的广西大新弄屯铅锌矿，拥有Pb+Zn金属量近60万吨，平均品位5.01%.

Nongtun Lead-Zinc Mine in Daxin, Guangxi, owned by the Group, has nearly 600,000 tons of Pb+Zn with an average grade of 5.01%.

投资开发建设的广西合浦古海温泉项目正在实施

Guhai Hot Spring Project in Hepu, Guangxi, invested and developed by the Group, is being implemented.

在广西“十二五”农村饮水安全找水打井工程中战果卓著

The Group gained excellent results in the rural drinking water safety and water-seeking and well-drilling project of the “Twelfth Five-Year Plan” in Guangxi.

武鸣全域土地综合整治施工现场

Whole-land comprehensive improvement and construction in Wuming

隆安点灯山矿山生态修复综合治理项目

Diandeng Mountain mine ecological restoration comprehensive management project in Long 'an

对广西340余所中小学开展“北斗云”地质灾害预警监测。公司的北斗云工程监测系统，拥有广西地矿局首家地质信息技术与物联网融合的地质灾害自动监测平台, 运用了“物联网”、“互联网+”、“北斗卫星”等多项新技术，通过北斗卫星导航系统和物联网系统、大数据采集平台、无人机建模系统、位移分析系统、微波遥感监测三维预警分析系统等，建立自动监测物联网数据采集和分析平台，GIS的成果展示平台。从天、空、地对地质灾害隐患点进行一体化位移变形自动监测。

Early warning and monitoring of geological disasters in Beidou Cloud were carried out in more than 340 primary and secondary schools in Guangxi. The Group’s Beidou cloud engineering monitoring system has the first automatic monitoring platform for geological disasters in Guangxi Bureau of Geology and Mineral Prospecting and Exploitation, which integrates geological information technology and Internet of Things, and uses many new technologies such as IoT, Internet Plus and Beidou Satellite. Through Beidou satellite navigation system and IoT system, big data collection platform, UAV modeling system, displacement analysis system and microwave remote sensing monitoring three-dimensional early warning analysis system, the Group established a platform for automatic monitoring of IoT data collection and analysis, and a GIS results display platform. Integrated automatic monitoring of displacement and deformation of hidden points of geological disasters is carried out from the sky, air and ground.
