广西壮族自治区地球物理勘察院

Guangxi Geophysical Survey Institute

 

广西地球物理勘察院是集区域地质调查、地质矿产勘查、地球物理勘查、地球化学勘查、地灾调查与防治、农业地质调查、生态地质调查、水工环调查、工程勘察及施工、工程物探、测绘、化验分析及科研为一体的综合性地勘单位，现有在职职工328人，其中专业技术人员230余人，含正高级职称1人，副高级职称91人，中级职称93人，各类注册工程师28人。持有工程勘察专业类岩土工程（勘察、设计、物探测试监测检测）甲级，地质灾害防治危险性评估甲级，地质灾害勘查、设计、施工甲级；水文地质勘察、测绘乙级；地基与基础工程专业承包贰级；地基基础工程检测；工程勘察劳务类（工程钻探）等。拥有配套完善的物探、化探、地质、测绘、钻探、岩土工程、工程检测、化验分析、水工环、计算机制图等先进的仪器设备400多台套。

Guangxi Geophysical Survey Institute is a comprehensive geological prospecting institution integrating regional geological survey, geological and mineral exploration, geophysical survey, geochemical survey, geological disaster investigation and prevention, agricultural geological survey, ecological geological survey, hydrogeological, engineering and environmental geological survey, engineering survey and construction, engineering geophysical exploration, surveying and mapping, laboratory analysis and scientific research. The institute has 328 employees, including more than 230 professional and technical personnel, 1 professional-level senior engineer, 91 associate senior engineers, 93 middle titles and 28 various chartered engineers. The Institute has many qualifications, such as Grade A in geotechnical engineering (survey, design, geophysical testing and monitoring), Grade A in risk assessment of geological disaster prevention and control, and Grade A in geological disaster exploration, design and construction; Grade B in hydrogeological survey, surveying and mapping; Grade B in professional contracting of foundation and foundation engineering. It also has qualification in foundation engineering inspection; engineering survey services (engineering drilling), etc. The Institute has more than 400 sets of advanced instruments and equipment for geophysical survey, geochemical survey, geological survey, surveying and mapping, drilling, geotechnical engineering, engineering detection, laboratory analysis, hydrogeological, engineering and environmental geological survey and computer drawing.

2010年，被国土资源部授予国土资源系统西南抗旱找水打井工作先进集体，荣获2018年度广西十强地质勘查单位等50多项省部级以上集体荣誉。截至2022年底，荣获在地质找矿工作中作出重大贡献嘉奖、政府特殊津贴专家、全区国土资源系统先进个人、中国地质学会野外青年地质贡献奖—金罗盘奖、广西五一劳动奖章、广西技术能手、杰出工程师奖等省部以上荣誉表彰近80人次。开展各类项目荣获2020-2021年度国家优质工程奖、2022年广西科学技术进步奖三等奖等省部级以上奖项近90项，成功申请专利12项。

In 2010, the Institute was awarded as the advanced collective of drought-resistant water-seeking and well-drilling work in the southwest of the land and resources system by then the Ministry of Land and Resources, and won more than 50 collective honors at the provincial and ministerial levels, including the top ten geological exploration units in Guangxi in 2018. By the end of 2022, nearly 80 people have been honored including the award for making great contributions to geological prospecting, the special government allowance expert, the advanced individual of the Guangxi land and resources system, the Field Youth Geological Contribution Award, i.e. the Golden Compass Award, by Geological Society of China, Guangxi May Day Medal, Guangxi Technical Expert and Outstanding Engineer Award. The Institute won nearly 90 provincial and ministerial awards, including the 2020-2021 National Quality Engineering Award and the third prize of 2022 Guangxi Science and Technology Progress Award, and successfully applied for 12 patents.
