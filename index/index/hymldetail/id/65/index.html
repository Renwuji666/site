桂林理工大学地球科学学院

College of Earth Sciences of Guilin University of Technology

 

桂林理工大学地球科学学院拥有广西唯一的地质类一级学科博士点-地质资源与地质工程，2013年获一级学科博士学位授予权，2014年获地质资源与地质工程学科博士后科研流动站，2018年入选广西首批一流学科，2022年入选全球ESI前1%学科。学科历经长期沉淀与发展，形成有色金属隐伏矿床成矿理论与综合预测、地球物理勘查理论与信息技术、地球化学勘查理论与技术、区域性地质灾害成灾机理及防治四个主要学科方向，拥有有色金属矿产勘查与资源高效利用省部共建协同创新中心等国家级科研创新平台2个、教育部有色及贵金属隐伏矿床勘查工程研究中心等省部级科研创新平台5个、全国高校黄大年式教师团队和广西关键矿产资源深部勘查人才小高地等省部级以上科研团队5个；现有专任教师81人，其中博士生导师15人，包括：国家杰青、中国科学院“西部之光”人才等省部级人才12人。

The College of Earth Sciences of Guilin University of Technology has the only first-level discipline doctoral degree program of geology in Guangxi, i.e. the geological resources and geological engineering. In 2013, it was awarded the right of doctoral degree in the first-level discipline, and in 2014, it was awarded the post-doctoral research station in the discipline of geological resources and geological engineering. In 2018, it was selected into the first batch of first-class disciplines in Guangxi, and in 2022, it was selected into the top 1% of the global ESI disciplines. After long-term precipitation and development, the disciplines of the College have subdivided into four major disciplines: metallogenic theory and comprehensive prediction of hidden nonferrous metal deposits, geophysical exploration theory and information technology, geochemical exploration theory and technology, and disaster mechanism and prevention of regional geological disasters. The College has two national-level scientific research innovation platforms, such as the collaborative innovation center for nonferrous metal mineral exploration and efficient utilization of resources, five provincial-level scientific research innovation platforms, such as the exploration engineering research center for hidden nonferrous and precious metal deposits of the Ministry of Education, and 5 scientific research teams including the national-level Huang Danian-style teaching staff and deep exploration talents of Guangxi’s key mineral resources. At present, there are 81 full-time teachers, including 15 doctoral supervisors, and among them, there are 12 provincial and ministerial-level talents from the National Outstanding Youth and Western Star of China Academy of Sciences.

桂林理工大学地球科学学院立足广西和西部资源禀赋与地理区位特色，围绕有色及贵金属矿产勘查，建成了一支年龄与知识结构合理的高水平科研队伍，开展有色及贵金属隐伏矿床成矿理论与勘查新技术、新方法的科技攻关和成果转化工作。科研团队通过融合地质、地球化学和地球物理方法，构建了3类不同隐伏花岗岩成矿环境的矿床模型，研发了4项专门针对隐伏花岗岩位态探测和矿致异常识别的关键技术，成功推动了锡、钨、铅、锌、铌和钽等资源勘查从第一找矿空间（<500m）到第二找矿空间（500-1000m）的有效拓展和延伸。技术成果在区内一批国家级整装勘查区和危机矿山推广应用，取得了深部找矿和资源增储的重大突破，为广西新增金属量Sn 5.4万吨，WO3 20.24万吨，Pb+Zn 77.3万吨，Mo 1.27万吨，Ta2O5 0.1万吨，Nb2O5 0.2万吨，产生潜在经济效益353亿元。分别延长栗木和油麻坡矿山服务年限近15年和21年。

Based on the resource endowments and geographical location characteristics of Guangxi and western China, the College of Earth Sciences of Guilin University of Technology has built a high-level scientific research team with reasonable age and knowledge structure around the exploration of nonferrous and precious metal deposits, and carried out scientific and technological research and transformation of new technologies and methods for metallogenic theory and exploration of hidden nonferrous and precious metal deposits. By combining geological, geochemical and geophysical methods, the scientific research team has constructed three types of deposit models with different metallogenic environments of hidden granite, and developed four key technologies specifically for detecting the position of hidden granite and identifying anomalies induced by ores, which successfully promoted the effective expansion of resources exploration of tin, tungsten, lead, zinc, niobium and tantalum from the first prospecting space (< 500m) to the second prospecting space (500-1000m). The technical achievements have been popularized and applied in a number of state-level integrated exploration areas and crisis mines in Guangxi, and a major breakthrough has been made in deep prospecting and resource reserves increasing. The newly added metal amounts in Guangxi are 54,000 tons of Sn, 202,400 tons of WO3, 773,000 tons of Pb+Zn, 12,700 tons of Mo, 501,000 tons of Ta2O and 502,000 tons of Nb2O, resulting in potential economic benefits of 35.3 billion yuan. It has extended the service life of chestnut mine and Youmapo mine by nearly 15 years and 21 years respectively.
