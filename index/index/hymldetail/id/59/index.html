广西森合高新科技股份有限公司

Guangxi Senhe High Technology Co., Ltd.

（下称“森合高科”）是专业从事环保型黄金选矿技术产品研发、生产和销售的高新科技企业。森合高科生产的金蝉®环保型黄金选矿剂，是“环保提金”专利品牌，可取代氰化钠在黄金工业中的应用。该产品被中华人民共和国工业和信息化部认定为第三批专精特新“小巨人”企业、入选自然资源部《矿产资源节约和综合利用先进适用技术目录（2022年版）》、荣获“中国黄金协会科学技术一等奖”“广西科技成果鉴定”“广西科技创新基金奖”等荣誉。目前，森合高科与中国黄金集团、山东黄金集团、山东招金集团、紫金矿业集团及越南越老公司等国内外大型黄金生产企业建立了长期合作关系，（产品）在提高黄金生产企业经济效益的同时，大大缓解了黄金工业发展对人类生存环境带来的危害，真正实现“绿色矿山，环保提金”！森合高科将以现代化的生产管理、物流运输及专业化的技术团队服务于全球客户!

Guangxi Senhe High Technology Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as “Senhe Hi-Tech”) is a high-tech enterprise specializing in the research, development, production and sales of environmental-friendly gold dressing technology products. Jin Chan®, an environmental-friendly gold dressing agent produced by Senhe Hi-Tech, is a patented brand of “environmental protection for gold extraction” and can replace the application of sodium cyanide in gold industry. This product was selected into the Catalogue of Advanced and Applicable Technologies for Saving and Comprehensive Utilization of Mineral Resources (2022 Edition) by the Ministry of Natural Resources of China, and Senhe Hi-Tech was recognized by the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology of China as one of the third batch of specialized and innovative “little giant” enterprises, and won such awards as the first prize of science and technology from China Gold Association, appraisal of scientific and technological achievements in Guangxi and Guangxi Science and Technology Innovation Fund Award. At present, Senhe Hi-Tech has established long-term cooperative relations with large domestic and foreign gold producers, such as China National Gold Group, Shandong Gold Group, Shandong Zhaojin Group, Zijin Mining Group and Vietnam-Laos Company of Vietnam. The product not only improves the economic benefits of gold producers, but also greatly alleviates the harm caused to the environment by the development of the gold industry, truly realizing environmental-friendly gold extraction. Senhe Hi-Tech will serve customers all over the world with modern production management, logistics and transportation and professional technical team!

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

产品介绍：金蝉®环保型黄金选矿剂

Product Introduction: Jin Chan® Environmental-friendly Gold Dressing Agent

金蝉®环保型黄金选矿剂是广西森合高新科技股份有限公司自主研发的可取代氰化钠用于黄金选矿的高新科技产品，是“环保提金”专利产品；产品在不改变原有“氰化提金”工艺及设备的情况下，可替代氰化钠用于黄金生产；同时具有低毒环保、回收率高、稳定性好、操作方便、回收快、用量少、成本低、储存运输方便等优点。

Jin Chan® environmental-friendly gold dressing agent is a high-tech product used in gold dressing, which is independently developed by Senhe High-tech as its patent product of “environmental-friendly gold dressing” and can replace sodium cyanide; the product can replace sodium cyanide in gold production without changing the original technology of “gold extraction by cyanide” and the equipment; at the same time, it has the advantages of low toxicity, environmental-friendly, high recovery rate, good stability, easy operation, fast recovery, less dosage, low cost, convenient storage and transportation.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

产品规格形态：灰白色粉末状或颗粒状

产品包装及规格：袋装, 每袋产品净重25KG±0.2KG（图示），为防止假冒伪劣产品流入市场，于2023年2月起生产每一批次的产品，在原包装上加印防伪码。客户可通过扫描防伪码了解产品真伪、防伪码所属产品、查询次数、产品介绍、规格形态及公司信息。产品一袋一码，产品的客户流向信息、所属批次将备案于我司，进行全程追溯。

Product form: greyish-white powder or granule

Product packaging and specifications: bagged, the net weight of each bag is 25kg±0.2kg (as shown in the picture). Each batch of products produced after February 2023 will be printed anti-counterfeiting codes on the original packaging to avoid shoddy products from entering the market. Customers can learn about the authenticity of products, products to which the security code belongs, inquiry times, product introduction, specifications and company information by scanning the security code. Each package of products has a code, and the product flow information and batches are registered with our company for full traceability.

工厂实力 Factory Strength

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

森合高科金蝉®环保型黄金选矿剂的工厂占地面积超100亩，配以先进的自动化生产线，从投料、进料、混料、冷却、破碎到包装实现全程自动化；厂区以严格的质量管理要求控制每个流程节点，以高量产和充足的货源，保障超30多个国家的全球市场供应。

The factory of Jin Chan® environmental-friendly gold dressing agent covers an area of over 100 mu, and is equipped with advanced automatic production lines to realize full automation from feeding, mixing, cooling, crushing to packaging. The factory controls each process node with strict quality management requirements, and ensures the global market supply of more than 30 countries with high mass production and sufficient supply.
