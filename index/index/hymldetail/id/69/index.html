广西壮族自治区二七二地质队

The 272 Geological Team of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region

广西壮族自治区二七二地质队/广西有色勘察设计研究院，两块牌子，一套人员，始建于1960年4月，为一家拥有15类40项资质（其中甲级13项）、专业搭配合理齐全的技术密集服务型区直事业单位，拥有各类专业技术人员近400人（其中：注册岩土师、规划师、结构师、建造师、测绘师、不动产登记代理人、安全师等执业资格人员近83人；高级及以上职称人员92人，中级职称129人，技术员及初级职称162人），是广西高新技术企业、广西五一劳动奖获得单位、广西区“青年文明号”集体、全国工程勘察与岩土行业诚信单位、广西优秀勘察设计院、广西优秀规划设计单位。是广西最早的测绘队伍之一，也是广西首家测绘航空摄影甲级单位，拥有6项甲级2项乙级测绘资质，曾荣获国家人事部、国家测绘局颁发的“全国测绘先进班组”称号。近年来，获省部级科技进步奖、有色部级或广西优秀工程勘察设计项目奖、国家或广西优质测绘地理信息产品(工程)奖、广西地矿局优秀工程勘察设计项目奖、广西自然科学论文奖等奖项280多项，主编行业规范2项，参编行业规范8项，自主研发软件40余项（已获得软著和科技成果登记25项），自主科研成果获得专利4项。单位是广西地球物理学会、广西勘察设计协会、广西土地学会、广西测绘学会、广西地质学会、广西建设工程质量检测试验协会等20个行业协会、学会的副会长单位或常务理事、理事或会员单位，连续多年被南宁市政府评为“先进集体”、并荣获南宁市“重合同守信用企业”称号，在社会上享有良好的声誉。

The 272 Geological Team of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, also known as Guangxi Nonferrous Survey and Design Institute, was founded in April 1960. It is a technology-intensive service-oriented provincial public institution with reasonable and complete professional collocation, and 15 categories and 40 qualifications (including 13 Grade A qualifications). It has nearly 400 professional and technical personnel (including 83 registered geotechnical engineers, planners, structural engineers, constructors, surveyors, real estate registration agents and safety engineers, 92 people with senior and above professional titles, 129 with intermediate professional titles, 162 people being technicians and with junior professional titles). It is a high-tech enterprise in Guangxi that has won the May Day Award in Guangxi and has been awarded many titles, such as the collective of Youth Civilization in Guangxi, the National Integrity Institution of Engineering Survey and Geotechnical Industry, the Excellent Survey and Design Institute in Guangxi, and the Excellent Planning and Design Unit in Guangxi. It is one of the earliest surveying and mapping teams in Guangxi, and also the first Grade A institution of surveying and mapping aerial photography in Guangxi. It has 6 Grade A and 2 Grade B surveying and mapping qualifications, and was awarded the title of National Advanced Surveying and Mapping Team by the former Ministry of Personnel and the State Bureau of Surveying and Mapping. In recent years, it has won more than 280 awards, such as the scientific and technological progress award of provincial and ministerial level, the excellent engineering survey and design project award of ministerial level or by Guangxi, national or Guangxi high-quality surveying and mapping geographic information product (project) award, excellent engineering survey and design project award of Guangxi Bureau of Geology and Mineral Prospecting and Exploitation, Guangxi natural science paper award, etc., edited 2 industry standards, participated in 8 industry standards, developed more than 40 independent software (registered 25 soft works and scientific and technological achievements), and obtained 4 patents for independent scientific research achievements. It is the vice-president unit or executive director, director or member unit of 20 trade associations and societies, such as Guangxi Geophysical Society, Guangxi Survey and Design Association, Guangxi Land Society, Guangxi Surveying and Mapping Society, Guangxi Geological Society and Guangxi Construction Engineering Quality Testing and Testing Association. It has been rated as the “Advanced Collective” by Nanning Municipal Government for many years and won the title of “Trustworthy Enterprise” in Nanning, enjoying a good reputation in society.
