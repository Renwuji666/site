南方锰业集团有限责任公司

Introduction to South Manganese Group Limited

 

南方锰业集团有限责任公司(香港联交所股票简称:南方锰业，股票代码:HK1091)是集采、选、冶于一体的锰系产品生产与研发大型跨国企业集团，总资产100亿元，员工7200人，2010年在香港联交所主板上市，全国首家在香港上市的资源型锰业企业，在国际国内锰行业具有重要的影响力。2022年，南方锰业集团连获“国家技术创新示范企业、国家知识产权示范企业、国家专精特新'小巨人’、全国和谐劳动关系创建示范企业”等4项国家级荣誉。

South Manganese Group Limited (stock abbreviation in Hong Kong Stock Exchange: South Manganese, stock code: HK1091) is a large multinational enterprise group that produces and develops manganese products, integrating mining, selecting and metallurgy, with total assets of 10 billion yuan and 7200 employees. It was listed on the main board of Hong Kong Stock Exchange in 2010, and is the first resource based manganese enterprise listed in Hong Kong in China and has important influence in the international and domestic manganese industry. In 2022, South Manganese Group won four national honors, including “National Technological Innovation Demonstration Enterprise, National Intellectual Property Demonstration Enterprise, National Specialized and Innovation ‘Little Giant’, and National Harmonious Labor Relations Creation Demonstration Enterprise”.

南方锰业集团锰矿资源丰富，积极实施“走出去”发展战略，在海外建立了高品质矿产资源基地，产业布局辐射广西、贵州、加蓬等地。经过多年发展在全国乃至全球构成了四大鲜明特色和品牌:一是锰矿资源丰富。在国内外拥有的已探明锰矿资源储量位居全国前列，锰矿开采能力位居亚洲前列，其中采矿量350万吨/年，位居亚洲第一。二是打造了全球领先的锰产业链和锰系产品线。核心产品主要有:电解金属锰、电解二氧化锰、硫酸锰、高纯硫酸锰、锰酸锂、镍钴锰酸锂三元材料、硅锰合金、锰桃、放电锰粉等。三是产品规模居行业领先。电解金属锰产能23万吨，位居中国第二;电解二氧化锰产能16.5万吨，位居中国第一;锰酸锂产能2万吨，中国领先;硅锰合金产能24万吨，南方领先。四是打造了领军行业的一流锰业技术和人才团队。打造了以全国劳模陈奇志等3位享受国务院特殊津贴专家为代表的锰行业领军人才队伍。

The South Manganese Group is rich in manganese resources. It actively implements the “go global” development strategy, establish overseas high-quality mineral resource bases, and deploy industries in Guangxi, Guizhou, Gabon and other places. After years of development, the Group has formed four distinctive characteristics and brands in China and even around the world: Firstly, it is rich in manganese resources. The proven reserves of manganese ore resources at home and abroad rank first in China, with the mining volume of 3.5 million tons per year, ranking first in Asia. Secondly, it has built a world leading manganese industry chain and manganese product line. The core products mainly include electrolytic manganese metal, electrolytic manganese dioxide, manganese sulfate, high-purity manganese sulfate, manganate, nickel cobalt lithium manganate ternary materials, silicon manganese alloy, manganese peach, discharge manganese powder, etc. Thirdly, the product scale is leading in the industry. The capacity of electrolytic manganese metal is 230,000 tons, ranking second in China; the capacity of electrolytic manganese dioxide is 165,000 tons, ranking first in China; the production capacity of lithium manganate is 20,000 tons, leading in China; The capacity of silicon manganese alloy is 240,000 tons, leading in the south. Fourthly, it has built a first-class technology and talent team of the manganese industry which has created a leading talent team in the manganese industry represented by three experts who enjoy the special allowance of the State Council, including the national model worker Chen Qizhi.

根据“十四五”发展规划，南方锰业集团制定了重点发展“矿山和传统产品、新能源材料、合金材料、供应链物流、固废资源化综合利用”五大业务板块的布局。集团将强力打造国家级绿色矿山，扎实推进产品纵向一体化战略，助推中国锰产业实现绿色高质量发展，发展成为全球最具核心竞争力的全产业链锰业集团!

According to the 14th Five-Year Plan, South Manganese Group focuses on the development of five business sectors: mining and traditional products, new energy materials, alloy materials, supply chain logistics and comprehensive utilization of solid waste resources. The Group will build a national-level green mine, promote the vertical integration strategy of products, support the green and high-quality development of China’s manganese industry, and develop into the world’s most competitive manganese industry group with the entire industry chain.
