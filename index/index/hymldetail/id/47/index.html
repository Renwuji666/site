中国—东盟地学合作中心

China-ASEAN Geosciences Cooperation Center

 

一、建设背景

I. Background

中国—东盟地学合作中心于2018年12月12日在广西南宁揭牌成立，是自然资源部中国地质调查局、广西壮族自治区人民政府与东盟各国地质矿产部门联合共建的区域性国际地学矿业合作平台，也是广西重点支持创建的“100个国家级创新平台”之一。

China-ASEAN Geosciences Cooperation Center (hereinafter referred to as “the Center”) was co-founded on December 12, 2018 in Nanning, Guangxi by the government of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, China Geological Survey (CGS), geological and mining departments of ASEAN countries. It is a regional and international geosciences and mining cooperation platform, and is also one of the “100 National Innovative Platforms” with great support from Guangxi.

 

 

2013年，习近平主席在印尼国会的演讲中郑重提出“携手建设中国—东盟命运共同体”的倡议。

In 2013, President Xi Jinping proposed the initiative of “Building a China-ASEAN Community with a Shared Future” in his speech to Indonesia Parliament.

 

 

 

2017年，国际地科联、CCOP组织以及中国、柬埔寨、印度尼西亚、老挝、马来西亚、菲律宾、泰国、越南8国签署共同倡议书，倡议各方共同促进中国—东盟地学合作，增进中国与东盟各国地质矿产领域对话交流，推动中国与东盟各国地球科学互利共赢、共同发展。

In 2017, International Union of Geological Sciences (IUGS), CCOP, China, Cambodia, Indonesia, Laos, Malaysia, the Philippines, Thailand and Vietnam signed a joint initiative, advocating all parties to jointly promote China-ASEAN geosciences cooperation, strengthen exchanges between China and ASEAN countries in the field of geology and mining, and promote win-win results and geosciences development between China and ASEAN countries.

 

 

2018年，李克强总理在第21届中国—东盟国家领导人会议上提出中国愿与东盟“共建地学合作中心，助力绿色经济和可持续发展”。

In 2018, Premier Li Keqiang stated at the 21st China-ASEAN Summit that China was willing to build a geosciences cooperation center for boosting green economy and sustainable development” with ASEAN.

 

 

2018年11月15日，中国—东盟地学合作中心在自然资源部、广西自治区政府、中国地质调查局和东盟各国地质部门的共同见证下，在广西南宁正式揭牌。

On November 15, 2018, the Center was inaugurated in Nanning, Guangxi, in the presence of the Ministry of Natural Resources of China, the government of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, CGS and the geological departments of ASEAN countries.

 

  

2018年12月12日，中国—东盟地学合作中心挂牌成立。中国地质调查局，广西自然资源厅、广西科技厅有关领导与广西地矿局领导班子共同参加中心挂牌仪式。

On December 12, 2018, the Center was established. Relevant leaders of CGS, Department of Natural Resources of Guangxi, Department of Science and Technology of Guangxi, and the leading body of Guangxi Bureau of Geology & Mineral Prospecting & Exploitation jointly attended the opening ceremony of the Center.

二、中心职能

II. Function

中心致力于推进区域地学矿业一体化发展，构建中国—东盟地学命运共同体，支撑服务区域经济可持续发展。成立以来，中心充分发挥平台作用，取得了丰硕的合作成果。主要包括：与东盟国家合作实施了多个地质调查项目，组织完成援建柬埔寨国家地质实验室项目，建立了东盟地学数据库，推进东盟地学国别研究成果转化，升级改造和运营中国—东盟矿业信息服务平台，成立广西海外矿业产业联合会，设立中心境外办事机构等，为地勘单位和矿业企业提供精准服务。

The Center is committed to promoting the integrated development of regional geosciences and mining, and building a community of geosciences with a shared future to support the sustainable development of regional economy. Since its establishment, the Center has given full play to its role as a cooperation platform and achieved fruitful cooperation results, mainly including: it has carried out a number of geological survey projects in cooperation with ASEAN countries; organized and completed the project of assisting in building the National Geological Laboratory of Cambodia; established ASEAN geological database; promoted the transformation of geological research results of ASEAN member states; upgraded and operated the China-ASEAN Mining Information Service Platform; established Guangxi Overseas Mining Association; set up overseas offices, etc. The Center provides accurate services for geological prospecting units and mining enterprises.

 

1.开展面向东盟地区的地质矿产调查、地学研究合作、矿业合作、地学能力建设、地球科学重大问题联合攻关；

1. Carry out geological and mineral surveys, geosciences research cooperation, mining cooperation, geosciences capacity building, and joint research on major geosciences issues in ASEAN;

 

2.承担中国—东盟地学矿业信息大数据平台建设工作；

2. Undertake the construction of China-ASEAN geosciences and mining big data platform;

 

3.参与编制中国—东盟地学合作中长期发展规划及年度工作计划；

3. Participate in the formulation of medium- and long-term development plans and annual work arrangements for China-ASEAN geosciences cooperation;

 

4.面向东盟地区、辐射“一带一路”沿线其他各国，着力打造建成中国—东盟矿业领域“政策研究与高官对话、地学研究与科技创新、矿业投资与技术合作、人才培养与智库建设、成果服务与信息共享”等5大平台；

4. Efforts will be made to build five platforms of policy communication and official meeting, mining investment and technical cooperation, geoscientific research and technological innovation, achievement service and information sharing, and talent training and think tanks construction for the ASEAN region and other countries along the “Belt and Road”;

 

5.推进区域地学矿业一体化发展，构建中国—东盟地学命运共同体，支撑服务区域经济可持续发展。

5. Promote the integrated development of regional geosciences and mining, build a China-ASEAN geosciences community with a shared future, and support the sustainable development of the regional economy.

 

三、平台建设

III. Platform Construction

1. 中国—东盟地学合作中心东盟学院成立

1. Founding of ASEAN College of China-ASEAN Geosciences Cooperation Center

 

2019年11月15日，在“中国—东盟地学合作中心”合作共建框架下，由中心联合成都理工大学、中国地质大学（武汉）共同组建的“中国—东盟地学合作中心东盟学院”在2019年（第十届）中国—东盟矿业合作论坛上揭牌成立。未来5年，将为东盟国家提供100个来华攻读地学、矿业、测试技术等专业硕士、博士学位的留学生名额，提供500个来华短期研修与技术培训名额，切实提升东盟国家地学专业人员的能力建设和技术水平。

On November 15, 2019, under the joint construction framework of the “China-ASEAN Geosciences Cooperation Center”, the ASEAN College of China-ASEAN Geosciences Cooperation Center, sponsored by the Center in collaboration with Chengdu University of Technology and China University of Geosciences, Wuhan, was inaugurated in the 10th China-ASEAN Mining Cooperation Forum in 2019. In the next five years, it will provide opportunities for 100 ASEAN talents to study for master and doctoral degrees in geosciences, mining, detection technology and other majors in China, and for 500 ASEAN talents with short-term study and skills training to improve the capabilities and technical level of ASEAN geosciences professionals.

 

建立与东盟国家地学人才培养机制，开展专业技术和职业技能培训，提升东盟国家有色金属、黑色金属、非金属及贵金属等各类元素的测定能力，构建中国与东盟国家联合培养地学人才的新型培养模式。截至目前，已为东盟国家地球科学、地质调查、矿产资源评价、测试分析领域培养来华留学生196人，其中，本科生41名，硕士106名，博士48名，博士后1名。

The Center has established a geoscientific talents cultivation mechanism for ASEAN countries, providing professional and vocational skill training, promoting determination ability of nonferrous metals, ferrous metals, nonmetals and precious metals, and developing a new cultivating mode for geoscientific talents of China and ASEAN countries. So far, the Center has provided training of geosciences, geological survey, mineral resource assessment, testing and analysis for 196 international students, including 41 undergraduates, 106 master’s students, 48 doctors and 1 post doctor.
