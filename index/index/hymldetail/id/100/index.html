广西壮族自治区第六地质队

The Sixth Geological Team of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region

广西壮族自治区第六地质队成立于1954年，是广西地质矿产勘查开发局下属驻广西贵港市的区直正处级事业单位，主要承担国家和自治区基础性、公益性地质调查和战略性矿产勘查及商业性地质矿产勘查等。现拥有地质矿产、工程地质、水文地质、环境地质、资源工程、测绘、物探、化探、实验测试、探矿等各类专业技术人员250人，其中，教授级高级工程师1人、高级工程师51人。拥有工程勘察专业甲级、地基基础施工乙级、地质实验测试乙级、地质钻（坑）探乙级及液体矿产勘查、水工环地质调查、测绘、物化探等相关资质。国际著名地球物理学家黄大年同志早年曾在我队从事物探工作。

The Sixth Geological Team of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, founded in 1954, is a regional institution under the Guangxi Bureau of Geology and Mineral Prospecting and Exploitation in Guigang, Guangxi. It mainly undertakes basic and public welfare geological survey, strategic mineral exploration and commercial geological mineral exploration of the Country and Guangxi. At present, there are 250 professional and technical personnel in geology and mineral resources, engineering geology, hydrogeology, environmental geology, resource engineering, surveying and mapping, geophysical exploration, geochemical exploration, experimental testing, prospecting, etc., including 1 professor-level senior engineer and 51 senior engineers. It has a series of professional qualifications of Grade A in engineering investigation, Grade B in foundation construction, Grade B in geological experiment testing, and Grade B in geological drilling (pit) exploration, as well as the qualifications in liquid mineral exploration, hydraulic environmental geological survey, mapping, physical and chemical exploration. Comrade Huang Danian, an internationally renowned geophysicist, was engaged in the geophysical exploration work in the Team in his early years.

建队六十多年来，我队为国家共探明矿床192处，探获博白县油麻坡钨钼矿床（大型）、平南县大洲稀土矿床（大型）、贵港市龙头山金矿床（大型）等大、中型矿床73处；荣获中国有色金属工业科学技术奖（一等奖）及找矿成果奖等省部级地质勘查、科研成果奖30多项。

Over the past 60 years, the Team has explored and proved 192 deposits for the country, including 73 large and medium-sized deposits such as Youmapo Tungsten and molybdenum deposit (large) in Bobai County, Dazhou rare earth deposit (large) in Pingnan County and Longtoushan gold deposit (large) in Guigang City. It has won more than 30 provincial and ministerial geological exploration and scientific research achievements awards such as the Science and Technology Award (First Prize) of China Nonferrous Metals Industry and the Prospecting Achievement Awards.

我队紧跟国家“一带一路”发展战略，落实广西地矿局“走出去”发展目标，近十年间先后派出20余批专家团队共56人赴老挝、印度尼西亚、柬埔寨、越南等东南亚国家和非洲津巴布韦、刚果、肯尼亚等国家为矿山企业地质勘查提供技术服务，其中在肯尼亚巴林戈硅藻土矿地质勘查项目中提交硅藻土大型矿床一处，取得较好的找矿成果。

The Team closely adheres to the national development strategy of the “Belt and Road” initiative, implements the development goal of “going global” of Guangxi Bureau of Geology and Mineral Prospecting and Exploitation, and has sent more than 20 expert teams of a total of 56 people to Laos, Indonesia, Cambodia, Vietnam and other Southeast Asian countries and Zimbabwe, Congo, Kenya and other countries in Africa to provide technical services for geological exploration of mining enterprises in the past decade. It obtained good prospecting results in the overseas exploration work, for example, a large diatomite deposit being found in the geological exploration project of Baringo diatomite deposit in Kenya.

我队本着“诚信、合作、共赢”的宗旨，竭诚为社会各界提供优质服务。

With the mission of “integrity, cooperation and mutual benefits,” the Team is dedicated to providing high-quality services for all sectors of the community.
